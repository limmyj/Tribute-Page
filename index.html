Lee Chong Wei

Legendary Badminton Player from Malaysia

Lee Chong Wei, a prominent figure in the world of badminton, is known for his remarkable career and contributions to the sport.

Lee Chong Wei, born on October 21, 1982, is a retired Malaysian badminton player. He was one of the world's leading players and held the World No. 1 ranking for a record 349 weeks. Lee has won numerous titles, including three silver medals at the Olympics and multiple gold medals at the Commonwealth Games.
